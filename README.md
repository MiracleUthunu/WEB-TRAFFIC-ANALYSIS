
#  Global Web Traffic Analysis

**Power BI Business Intelligence Project**

## Project Context

This project explores global web performance from a strategic and decision-making perspective.

Rather than focusing only on traffic volume, the analysis evaluates **traffic quality, engagement strength, and market efficiency** to uncover hidden performance gaps across domains and countries.

The goal was to move beyond surface-level metrics and assess how effectively traffic translates into meaningful engagement.

---

## Analytical Focus

This analysis was designed to answer key business questions:

* Are high-traffic domains actually high-performing?
* Which markets generate scalable and sustainable engagement?
* Where is traffic being underutilized?
* How can engagement quality influence competitive positioning?

---

## Methodology

* Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
* Built calculated performance indicators using DAX
* Designed comparative domain benchmarking logic
* Created market-level segmentation models
* Developed efficiency evaluation metrics
* Structured visuals to support executive-level storytelling

Special focus was placed on:

* Engagement quality vs traffic volume
* Market efficiency dynamics
* Cross-category behavioral differences
* Performance imbalance detection

---

## Strategic Insights

The analysis reveals that traffic quantity alone is not a reliable indicator of digital strength.

Some domains demonstrate strong volume but weaker engagement behavior, suggesting potential content misalignment or retention gaps.

Market comparison also highlights variations in traffic utilization efficiency, creating opportunities for optimization and targeted growth strategies.

This project emphasizes the importance of evaluating **performance quality, not just performance size.**
